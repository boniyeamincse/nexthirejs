## 6. Privacy Model

### Overall Discoverability

Use these values:

```text
PRIVATE
LINK_ONLY
PLATFORM_DISCOVERABLE
```

Meaning:

- `PRIVATE`: profile is visible only to the candidate and authorized internal processes.
- `LINK_ONLY`: future public profile may be accessible only through an explicit share link; this task stores the preference only.
- `PLATFORM_DISCOVERABLE`: future authorized companies/recruiters may discover the candidate through platform search; search is out of scope now.

Do not use a simple boolean because future policy needs at least these three states.

### Section Visibility

Use these values:

```text
HIDDEN
PLATFORM_ONLY
PUBLIC
```

Meaning:

- `HIDDEN`: only the candidate and required internal services may read the section.
- `PLATFORM_ONLY`: future authenticated/authorized company or recruiter views may read the section.
- `PUBLIC`: future public-profile views may read the section when overall discoverability permits it.

### Supported Sections

Implement settings for:

```text
BASIC_PROFILE
LOCATION_AND_PREFERENCES
EDUCATION
WORK_EXPERIENCE
SKILLS_AND_LANGUAGES
CERTIFICATIONS_AND_TRAINING
ACHIEVEMENTS_AND_LINKS
```

Do not add sections that are not yet implemented.

## 8. Business Rules

Implement these exact rules:

1. Only authenticated users with the `candidate` role can access this feature.
2. A candidate can read and update only their own privacy settings.
3. Each candidate has at most one privacy-settings row.
4. Request bodies must not accept `userId`.
5. Overall discoverability is required.
6. Every supported profile section must have one visibility value.
7. Unknown sections are rejected.
8. Duplicate section keys are rejected.
9. Missing section keys are rejected for full `PUT` updates.
10. Unsupported visibility values are rejected.
11. `PUBLIC` section visibility does not itself make the profile public; overall discoverability remains authoritative.
12. With overall discoverability `PRIVATE`, future external access must be denied regardless of section values.
13. With `LINK_ONLY`, platform discovery must remain disabled.
14. With `PLATFORM_DISCOVERABLE`, sections marked `HIDDEN` remain unavailable.
15. The backend is the source of truth for privacy decisions.
16. The frontend must not claim that public or company views already exist.
17. Suspended or deleted accounts cannot update privacy settings.
18. All timestamps use UTC.
19. Audit metadata must not include profile content.
20. Audit metadata may include old/new discoverability, changed section names, and policy version.
21. Unexpected failures must not expose Prisma or stack-trace details.
22. The update must be idempotent.
23. Privacy settings must not change candidate profile-completion percentage.
24. Existing profile data must not be deleted when hidden.
25. Privacy configuration must not modify any candidate-profile section records.
26. Git commit occurs only after all mandatory checks pass.
27. A blocked task must not create a commit.
